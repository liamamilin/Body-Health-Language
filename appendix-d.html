Appendix D: The Body-Health Loop Template

This appendix provides a practical template for working through any body-health issue or capacity problem using the complete body-health loop. Use this template as a structured journal entry, a daily check-in, or a problem-solving guide when a body signal or capacity concern arises.

Copy the template into a notebook, document, or note-taking app. Fill in the fields relevant to your situation. Not all fields are required for every use; use the sections that serve your current need.
